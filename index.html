About Me

Welcome to my website. My name is Kolton Decker and I am an aspiring full stack web developer. I grew up in a small town in Southwest Kansas. I enjoyed playing sports and video games growing up as well as hanging with friends and going on wild adventures.

I attended and graduated from Kansas State University in 2017 with a Bachelors Degree in Mechanical Engineering and have been working in the natural gas and power industry for the last 3 years. I currently work as a Project Manager for Panhandle Eastern Pipeline Company doing pipeline maintenance and infrastructure improvement projects.

My current goal is to graduate KU Coding Bootcamp and land a position as a full stack web developer where I can gain hands-on industry experience and continue to learn for with an ever-changing world, I must never stop learning. On this website you will find some of the projects I have built. I will continue to update this portfolio as I progress through my career.
